Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files containing a string of characters that can be placed on your computer or mobile device that uniquely identify your browser or device.

Learn about cookies.

How does OpenAgua use cookies?

OpenAgua uses a single authentication cookie. This cookie allows you to log in to secure areas on the site, and stay logged in.

OpenAgua does not use third-party cookies.

How long will cookies stay on my device?

The cookie OpenAgua uses is “persistent”, and will stay on your computer or mobile device until they expire or are deleted.

How to control and delete cookies

Managing cookies is unique to each browser. To find specific instructions for your browser, do a simple web search (e.g., "how to delete cookies in chrome").

Please note that if you disable cookies entirely, you will not be able to log in.
